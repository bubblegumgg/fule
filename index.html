About Me

I am Youxuan Wang, a Technical Art–focused applicant with a background in digital media and real-time environment production. My work sits at the intersection of visual design and technical implementation, with a strong emphasis on Unreal Engine workflows and asset-based scene construction.

Through my studies and projects, I have focused on building complete real-time environments, from modeling and material creation to in-engine layout, lighting, and sequencing. I am particularly interested in how technical decisions—such as modular design, material workflows, and lighting setup—shape both performance and visual storytelling.

Technical Skill Overview

  • Real-Time Engines: Unreal Engine (Blueprint basics, Sequencer, level assembly, lighting) Maya (basic Python)
  • Rendering & Materials： Node-based material creation (Unreal Engine, Substance Designer)
  • Substance Painter for texture refinement and PBR workflows
  • Asset Creation Maya (hard-surface modeling, UV layout, Arnold rendering)
  • Modular asset creation for real-time environments

Astrology (Personal Interest & Structured Thinking)

My passion as a developer lies in reverse-engineering complex systems and identifying underlying mathematical order. Astrology, for me, is an extension of this pursuit: it treats celestial mechanics as a vast, observable dataset governed by pure formulae.

This interest led me to develop a simple Horoscope Test (linked below). The project uses clean, raw code to simulate basic planetary movements and apply simple correlation logic to provide a mood forecast. This endeavor is a philosophical quest for pattern recognition and the hidden architecture of human experience, directly reinforcing the structured, logic-first methodology I bring to all my technical projects.
