03. Narrative Concept

As technology becomes increasingly advanced, I feel that the collective subconscious of humanity is growing lonelier. This idea inspired me to create this character. It appears helpless—perhaps even lost—uncertain of its place in the vast universe. Its subconscious manifests in the environment around it: a cold, industrial alleyway that feels abandoned.

I used a combination of cool and warm lighting to enrich the visual atmosphere while revealing the character’s inner state. It longs for warmth yet simultaneously retreats from it, echoing the emotional paradox expressed in Strait is the Gate: “When you are by my side, my heart is shattered. But when away from you, it cannot survive.”

04. Aniamtion

Fire in the Alley

I created a character animation sequence in Unreal Engine using a workflow that combined AI-assisted modeling and traditional animation tools. First, I generated the base 3D character model in Tencent Hunyuan 3D by importing images of a skeletal figure. After exporting the AI-generated model as an OBJ file, I uploaded it to Mixamo to retarget and generate multiple animation clips. I then exported those animations as FBX files. In Unreal Engine, I imported both the character model and the corresponding animation files, ensuring they shared the same skeleton. I created a new Level Sequence and arranged the Mixamo animation clips in the desired order to form a complete, continuous 10-second animation.
