Adventurer's Desk

A Maya-based environment study showcasing texturing workflow in Substance 3D Painter, supported by custom lighting and Arnold rendering. The scene is built to demonstrate material accuracy, controlled surface detailing, and a fully authored lookdev process through carefully crafted shaders and lighting setups.

03. Narrative Concept

I imagined this character as a world-traveling adventurer who likes to place the objects she collects from different places on her desk. While creating the scene, I drew a lot of inspiration from items on auction sites like Christie’s and Sotheby’s. I prefer objects to look natural and realistic, so I didn’t go for a stylized approach. Instead, I focused on keeping everything as close to real life as possible, especially in the details.
